Shell - Far Away

Far Away has been a timeline of sorts from the end of summer to early winter over my thoughts on my surroundings as I came to terms with how far away they began to feel. I feel far away from myself in the year prior and far away from the possibilities that next year brings. This distance has brought me closer to my memories of my identities over time. It has allowed me to recognize how deeply I'm embedded in the places I exist in.

Compiled over the course of four months, including ideas as old as six months ago. Recorded in my room, released January 1, 2022

The Shell: acoustic guitar, electric guitar, keyboard, vocals, mandolin, harmonica, noise, mastering.
